Cryptocurrencies of the World

Bitcoin (BTC)

The first and most popular cryptocurrency, created in 2009 by an unknown person using the name Satoshi Nakamoto

Ethereum (ETH)

A decentralized platform that enables smart contracts and decentralized applications (DApps) to run without downtime or fraud.

Binance Coin (BNB)

Used to pay fees on the Binance Exchance. BNB also powers the Binance Smart Chain ecosystem.

Ripple (XRP)

Bring a change of clothes to stay warm and dry during an exacuation. Designed for fast and low-cost international money transfers used by many banks and payment networks.
